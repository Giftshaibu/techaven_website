01

Who We Are

Techaven is a Malawi-based digital marketplace for electronics. We connect buyers with verified sellers, protect payments through escrow, and handle delivery in-house, so the buying process has accountability at every stage, not just at checkout.

02

Why Techaven Exists

Online electronics shopping in Malawi has always had a trust problem. Buyers don't know which sellers are legitimate. Sellers can't prove credibility to buyers they've never met. Money changes hands with no protection on either side.

Techaven exists to fix that. Not with promises, but with infrastructure: verified listings, structured payments, in-house delivery, and a dispute process that actually works.

03

Our Vision

To become Malawi's most trusted electronics marketplace, where buyers shop with confidence because the platform has done the verification work, and where honest sellers grow because trust is a structural advantage, not just a reputation they're hoping to build.

04

Our Mission

To make electronics shopping in Malawi safer and more transparent by connecting buyers with verified sellers through a mobile-first platform that handles the full transaction from discovery to delivery.

05

How It Works for Buyers

Browse products from verified sellers, compare options, and pay through escrow-protected checkout. Your payment is held until the product is delivered and confirmed. If something goes wrong, such as the wrong item, damaged delivery, or a no-show, there's a process to resolve it.

06

How It Works for Sellers

Get listed, reach buyers already looking for electronics, and sell through a structured channel with order management, delivery support, and a credibility system that rewards accurate listings and good service.

07

Why Customers Trust Us

Sellers are verified before listing. Payments are protected until delivery is confirmed. Delivery is managed in-house. Customer support handles problems rather than redirecting them. Every part of the transaction has a process behind it.

Why Sellers Work With Us

Techaven gives electronics businesses access to buyers in a payment-ready environment, without requiring them to manage every part of the sales process independently. Verified status signals credibility to buyers who've been burned by informal sellers before.

08

Built for Malawi's Digital Commerce Future

Malawi's mobile infrastructure, including smartphone access, Airtel Money, and TNM Mpamba, already supports digital commerce. What's been missing is a marketplace built around local transaction realities: mobile-first browsing, mobile money payments, and a trust model designed for a market where most buyers and sellers don't have a prior relationship.

Techaven is built for that market.

Our Mobile-First Shopping Experience

Most Malawians access the internet through their phones. Techaven is built phone-first: browsing, comparing, paying, and tracking orders are all designed for mobile use, not adapted from a desktop experience.

Your trusted marketplace for quality electronics. Simple, secure, and designed for you.
