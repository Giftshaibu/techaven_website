About Techaven

Malawi's Safer Way to Buy and Sell Electronics Online

Techaven is a Malawi-based electronics marketplace for people who want to buy online without sending money into the dark. We verify sellers, hold payments in escrow, and help with delivery until the order reaches the buyer.

Built for real transactions

Verified seller modelSellers are checked before they list products, so buyers are not relying on anonymous posts.
Escrow payment protectionPayment stays on hold until delivery is confirmed. That is the difference between paying a seller and hoping for the best.
Delivery supportTechaven stays involved after checkout, when many informal online deals usually start to get messy.

What Techaven Does

Techaven helps people in Malawi buy phones, laptops, accessories, and other electronics from sellers who have been checked before listing. Buyers can search, compare, pay through escrow, receive the product, and contact support if something goes wrong.

The point is simple: keep the deal in one place instead of spreading it across DMs, calls, screenshots, and hope.

Simple promise

Buyers deserve convenience without gambling with their money. Sellers also need a fair way to prove they are legitimate when a new customer asks, "Are you legit?"

02

Why Techaven Exists

Techaven exists because electronics are expensive, and a bad online deal can hurt. A phone can cost months of savings. A laptop can carry a student's schoolwork, a designer's clients, or a small business. When a buyer sends money to the wrong person, the loss is personal.

Trust cannot rest on screenshots, confident messages, or a seller saying "boss, don't worry." Techaven adds checks, payment protection, delivery updates, and a support team people can actually reach.

03

The problem we solve in Malawi

Many buyers in Malawi discover electronics through social media, referrals, and informal listings. That can work, but it also creates room for fake products, disappearing sellers, misleading photos, and payments with no protection.

Making Online Electronics Shopping Less Risky

Techaven gives buyers a cleaner path: checked sellers, product details in one place, protected checkout, delivery support, and a dispute process. Sellers get a more professional way to sell, especially when they are legitimate but still need to prove it to new customers.

04

Our Verified Seller and Escrow Model

Seller verification

Techaven checks sellers before they can list, which helps buyers avoid unknown or hard-to-trace sources.

Escrow protection

Techaven holds the payment during the transaction. The seller is paid after delivery is confirmed and the order matches the listing.

Support and disputes

If the product is not delivered, arrives damaged, or does not match the listing, the buyer can raise a support case for review.

05

Delivery Support Built Into the Marketplace

Delivery is where trust gets tested. Techaven helps coordinate the handover so buyers know how the product is moving from seller to customer. That matters in Malawi, where many online deals fall apart after payment because nobody clearly owns the delivery.

Techaven delivery starts with major city routes and expands as seller and delivery partner capacity grows. Current service coverage is listed on the Contact page.
