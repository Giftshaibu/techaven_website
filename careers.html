01

Why Work at Techaven?

Techaven is an early-stage digital commerce company building something that doesn't exist in Malawi yet: a full-stack electronics marketplace with verified sellers, escrow payments, and in-house delivery. That means the problems are real, the work affects real people, and the decisions made now matter in ways they won't once the company is larger.

If you want to join a company where your work is visible, where you'll handle problems that require you to think, and where the product you're building has direct social value, that's what Techaven is right now.

02

Our Mission

To make electronics shopping in Malawi safer and simpler by connecting buyers with verified sellers through a platform that handles the full transaction: discovery, payment, delivery, and support.

03

Internships and Graduate Opportunities

Techaven takes on interns and graduates in customer support, operations, marketing, technology, and seller relations. The work is hands-on from the start. If you're a recent graduate looking for a role where you learn by doing real work rather than shadowing, Techaven is worth looking at.

04

Teams

Support

Sales and Customer Support

Our support and sales teams handle the relationship between Techaven, its customers, and its sellers. That means answering real questions, resolving real problems, and improving the buying experience based on what customers actually report. It's not a scripted call centre role.

Operations

Delivery and Operations

Operations keeps the order process running from the moment a seller confirms stock to the moment a customer confirms receipt. If you like logistics, problem-solving under time pressure, and work that has a clear daily outcome, this team is the engine of what Techaven does.

Product

Technology and Product

The tech team builds and maintains the Techaven platform. Current stack is Node.js/Express on the backend with Supabase for the database and a React frontend. If you're a developer interested in building something used daily by real customers in Malawi, not just side projects, we want to hear from you.

Marketing

Marketing, Content, and Social Media

The marketing team explains what Techaven does, helps customers shop smarter, and builds the platform's presence in Malawi's digital spaces. If you write well, understand how Malawian audiences consume content online, and can produce material that's useful rather than just promotional, this team has real work for you.

Sellers

Seller Onboarding and Relationship Management

This team brings new electronics sellers onto the platform, helps them list products accurately, and supports them as they grow their digital sales channel. You'll work directly with electronics businesses across Malawi.

05

What You'll Learn Working With Techaven

How a marketplace business actually works, not in theory, but in practice. Seller management, customer experience, operations, digital marketing, product development, and the commerce mechanics that sit underneath all of it. Early-stage companies teach you faster than larger ones because you can't avoid the problems.

06

How Techaven Supports Young Talent in Malawi

We give early-career people real responsibilities, not shadow roles. If you produce good work, you'll be trusted with more of it. Malawi's digital economy is growing, and the people who understand it from the inside now will have an advantage as it does.

07

Job Openings — Watch This Space

We are not hiring at this moment, but roles will be listed here as Techaven grows. If you want to be notified when positions open, contact us through our official channels.

Roles coming as the team expands:

  • Customer Support Assistant Handles customer inquiries, order updates, and issue resolution.
  • Sales Representative / Seller Onboarding Officer Recruits and supports verified sellers joining the platform.
  • Delivery Operations Assistant Coordinates orders, delivery logistics, and supplier communication.
  • Social Media and Content Assistant Creates posts, educational content, and product highlights across Techaven's digital channels.
  • Product Listing / Platform Moderator Reviews listings for accuracy, flags issues, and keeps the marketplace organised.
  • Digital Marketing Assistant Runs campaigns, promotions, and online advertising to support platform growth.
  • Mobile App Tester Tests the Techaven mobile experience, reports bugs, and helps improve usability.
  • Junior Developer Supports platform development, fixes bugs, and contributes to feature improvements.
